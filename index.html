By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Wellness Data Consent

MyCalmSync allows users to log and track personal wellness information for self-awareness and lifestyle tracking. This may include: Mood logs, Sleep tracking,Hydration tracking,Energy levels,Screen time awareness.Other wellness entries provided by the user. This information is entered voluntarily by users and is used only to provide insights, trends, and tracking features within the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (securely hashed and stored by Supabase)
  • Profile information (bio, avatar selection)
  • Account creation date

This information is required to provide account access and authentication. MyCalmSync is completely free to use - there are no subscriptions or premium tiers.

1.2 Wellness Data

We collect wellness data you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Mood logs and emotional check-ins with timestamps
  • Mood followup responses and scale ratings
  • Mood analysis summaries and patterns
  • Breathing exercise sessions (duration, technique, completion)
  • Walking activity stats (duration, distance, steps)
  • Activity completion history and timestamps
  • Favorite activities and preferences

1.3 Wellness Tracking Data

If you choose to track wellness data, we collect:

  • Sleep quality records (duration, quality rating, notes, sleep quality scores)
  • Health metrics (steps, heart rate, calories, active minutes, weight)
  • Hydration logs (daily water intake, hydration goals, notes)
  • Screen time awareness logs (productive time, social media time, entertainment time)
  • Energy level logs (energy ratings 1-10, mood descriptors, activities, timestamps)
  • Sync timestamps and data source information
  • Historical trends and wellness insights

All wellness tracking features are completely optional.

1.4 User-Generated Content

We store content you create within the App:

  • Journal entries (text, timestamps, optional locks)
  • Gratitude journal entries
  • Reflection journal entries
  • Community chat messages and timestamps
  • Message reactions and read receipts
  • Books or written works you create and publish
  • Book chapters and reading progress
  • Bookmarks and saved content

1.5 Social & Community Data

We collect data related to social features:

  • Community interactions and participation

1.6 Gamification & Progress Data

We track your progress and achievements:

  • Coins earned through completed activities
  • Badges and achievements unlocked
  • Daily check-in streaks and consistency tracking
  • Activity completion counts and milestones
  • Progress towards wellness goals

1.7 App Usage & Activity Data

We track how you use the app to improve your experience:

  • Activity history (gratitude prompts, poems, reflections viewed)
  • Content preferences and favorites
  • Reading progress and book engagement
  • Daily activity rotation assignments
  • App usage patterns and feature engagement
  • Crisis resources accessed and viewed

1.8 Technical Information

We collect limited technical data for app functionality:

  • Device information and platform type
  • Session timestamps and authentication tokens
  • Log data for debugging and security
  • App performance metrics

1.9 Community Moderation Data

To maintain a safe community environment, we collect:

  • Message reports and flagged content
  • Moderation actions and timestamps
  • User-submitted safety concerns
  • Content that violates community guidelines

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App and its features
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts via Supabase
  • Enable journaling, wellness tracking, and mental health features
  • Track hydration, screen time, energy levels, and other wellness metrics
  • Facilitate community interactions and social connections
  • Provide personalized wellness recommendations and content
  • Track your progress, display statistics, and award achievements
  • Manage gamification features (coins, badges, streaks)
  • Send notifications and reminders based on your preferences
  • Analyze mood patterns and provide personalized insights
  • Rotate daily activities to provide variety
  • Improve functionality, user experience, and app features
  • Moderate community content and ensure user safety
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse or abuse
  • Provide crisis resources and emergency support when needed

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, wellness data, or health information with third parties for marketing purposes.
